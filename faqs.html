1.What kind of business support will be provided?

  • Handholding the team throughout the ideation process
  • Brainstorming the ideas
  • Idea validation
  • Helping the team to prepare comprehensive business case & KPIs
  • Connecting the team with industry experts, including alumni
  • Connecting the team with vendors / suppliers / professional firms
  • Providing professional and business inputs to the team
  • Holding lectures with industry experts / subject matter experts as speakers
  • Bringing in relevant and the best business practices
  • Helping the team in digital marketing & media coverage
  • Providing necessary infrastructure and technology to the team to set-up and run their business

2.What is the kind of physical infrastructure available?

We moved to the new campus in Rohini in July 2017. SIIF has one entire floor dedicated to incubating young entrepreneurs. We have six large halls to be deployed as co-working spaces, SIIF secretariat, and world class architecture including access to Wi-Fi, library, cafeteria and auditorium, all replete with central air-conditioning.

  • Auditorium

4.What are the advantages of being a part of our ecosystem?

SIIF offers you a combination of industry expertise, business networking opportunities, infrastructural facilities, funding, as well as hand holding during the entire incubation process. This kind of unique blend would give you a definitive competitive edge.

5.What kind of equity stake does the incubation centre expect?

The equity stake will vary for each startup, keeping in mind various factors.

6.Will there be any form of monetary capital contribution?

Yes, there will be funding for starting the business as well as meeting the running expenses of the business.

7.How is SIIF different from Venture Capital Funds?

A Venture Capitalist Fund would review the business and take the decision regarding whether to invest in you or not and then, a Venture Capitalist representative would sit on your Board to direct it. On the other hand, SIIF would be engaged with you whole heartedly right from the ideation stage onwards.

We endeavour to incubate the project till it is ready for series A funding; or if the project is shelved. Typically we see the engagement with SIIF to be of 1-3 years.

18.What if my project fails? Do I have to return the money invested by SIIF in my project?

The amount of money genuinely spent on business expenses of the venture under incubation (necessary evidences required) need not be returned.

19.Is SIIF a start-up cell within the college?

No, it is a distinct corporate entity constituted as a Section 8 company under the Companies Act, 2015.

20.Is SIIF a separate faculty/branch within the college?

No, it is not a faculty or department under the college. We co-coexist with SSCBS by the virtue of being a company promoted by SSCBS.

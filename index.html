About


SSCBS Innovation and Incubation Foundation (SIIF) is an outcome of Incubation Policy from Govt. of NCT, Delhi. Under this policy, initially 6 institutes from Delhi were selected to develop incubation centres within the institute, with an objective to provide a progressive and comprehensive platform to the entrepreneurs.

Who all can benefit through SIIF?


We are happy to engage with people from outside the SSCBS fraternity, who have strong passion and ideas to be on their own, to set up new ventures and to create new businesses.

Engagement Model


We are taking B2C as well as B2B approach. This means that while SIIF will incubate the individual teams for their specific projects, we would also create opportunities for existing incubators to leverage our ecosystem and our infrastructure to help their incubatees.

SIIF network


The quality of industry network gives an accelerator a cutting edge over others. SIIF has a strong network of industry experts, technology providers, venture capitalists / investors / financial institutions, faculty, trainers, and of course, the young entrepreneurs who can share their successes and failures.

Who all can benefit through SIIF?


Our platform is available to students, alumni, faculty and staff of SSCBS. However, we are happy to engage with people from outside the SSCBS fraternity, who have strong passion and ideas to be on their own, to set up new ventures and to create new businesses.



You can write to us at ceo-siif@sscbsdu.ac.in

Engagement Model


  • Handholding the team throughout the ideation process
  • Brainstorming the ideas
  • Idea validation
  • Helping the team to prepare comprehensive business case & KPIs
  • Connecting the team with industry experts, including alumni
  • Connecting the team with vendors / suppliers / professional firms
  • Providing professional and business inputs to the team
  • Holding lectures with industry experts / subject matter experts as speakers
  • Bringing in relevant and the best business practices
  • Helping the team in digital marketing & media coverage
  • Providing necessary infrastructure and technology to the team to set-up and run their business


We are taking B2C as well as B2B approach. This means that while SIIF will incubate the individual teams for their specific projects, we would also create opportunities for existing incubators to leverage our ecosystem and our infrastructure to help their incubatees.

You can write to us at ceo-siif@sscbsdu.ac.in

Facilities and Infrastructure


SIIF has a great environment, infrastructure and other facilities for young entrepreneurs to convert their ideas into successful business ventures.


In the new campus at Rohini, we have centrally air-conditioned large halls, conference rooms, meeting rooms, library, cafeteria, an auditorium with a capacity of 500 pax, supported with relevant technology, office support systems etc.


SIIF Network


The quality of industry network gives an accelerator a cutting edge over others. SIIF has a strong network of industry experts, technology providers, venture capitalists / investors / financial institutions, faculty, trainers, and of course, the young entrepreneurs who can share their successes and failures. We also have access to global pool of expertise and talent through The Indus Entrepreneurs(TiE), a not-for-profit international network of entrepreneurs, investors and industry experts. We would be glad to provide you an opportunity to be part of our network and help upcoming entrepreneurs.
